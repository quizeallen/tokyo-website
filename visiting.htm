Visiting Tokyo

Visa Requirements

Before embarking on your Tokyo adventure, ensure you meet the visa requirements:

  • Valid Passport: All foreign visitors must have a valid passport for the duration of their stay.
  • Visa Application: Complete the visa application form.
  • Passport Photo: Provide a recent color passport photo.
  • Birth Certificate: Include a certified copy of your birth certificate.
  • Onward or Return Tickets: Show proof of your travel plans.
  • Sufficient Funds: Have enough funds to cover your stay.

Where to Stay

Tokyo offers a wide range of accommodation options to suit every budget and preference. Here are some popular areas to consider:

  1. Shinjuku: Vibrant and central, Shinjuku is home to skyscrapers, shopping centers, and entertainment districts. Recommended stays include:
    • Hotel Sunroute Plaza Shinjuku: A sophisticated hotel with soundproofed rooms and easy access to transportation.
    • JR Kyushu Hotel Blossom Shinjuku: A stylish hotel near JR Shinjuku Station.
  2. Ginza: Known for luxury shopping and upscale dining, Ginza offers elegant hotels and a prime location.
  3. Shibuya: Famous for the iconic Shibuya Crossing, this area boasts trendy boutiques, restaurants, and nightlife.
  4. Asakusa: Home to the historic Sensō-ji Temple, Asakusa offers traditional ryokans and a glimpse of old Tokyo.

Getting Around

Tokyo’s efficient public transportation system makes exploring the city a breeze:

  • Trains and Subways: The best way to get around. Use a prepaid Suica or Pasmo card for seamless payments.
  • Buses: Reliable for short journeys in quieter suburbs.
  • Walking and Cycling: Explore neighborhoods by foot or bike.
  • Water Buses: Combine sightseeing with your commute on Tokyo’s water buses.

Estimated Costs

Budgeting for Tokyo depends on your preferences, but here’s a rough estimate:

  • Accommodation: $125 per night on average.
  • Flights: Around $1,500 per person.
  • Food, Drink & Activities: $100 per person per day.
  • Transportation: Approximately $5 per person per day.
  • Total Cost for a Week: $4,000 to $5,500 for two people.
